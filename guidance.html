Guidance & Reflection

The Quran repeatedly describes the qualities and deeds that lead toward Allah's mercy and Paradise, as well as behaviors it warns against. Below are some recurring themes, with references for further study.

A note on context: Each theme below is a brief summary intended to encourage reflection and further study — not a complete ruling or judgment on any individual. The Quran's verses should always be read within their full surah, alongside trusted tafsir (commentary) and scholarship, rather than in isolation.
