Staff Dashboard

Manage courses, paid videos, payment details, and live meetings. Changes go live for all visitors after you click "Publish Changes".

How it works: Fill in any of the panels below, then scroll down and press Publish Changes with your GitHub token. Everything is saved to the live website for all users.

💳 Payment Details

Shown to users when they want to unlock a paid video or enroll in a course.

🎓 Add a New Course

Appears on the Courses page next to the existing courses.

🎬 Add a Paid Video Lesson

Users see the title and price; they get the video link after they pay and you confirm.

📹 Create a Live Meeting

Generates a unique meeting code. Only users you give the code to can join. Meetings support recording and live translated captions (Arabic, English, Urdu, Indonesian, Bengali).

💬 User Feedback

Feedback that users send from the site arrives at the site email inbox: ammarwalidyounis@gmail.com. Check it there and reply directly.
