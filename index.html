Common Questions


What can I find in the Quran Library?

All 114 surahs with their Arabic names, meanings, number of verses, and whether they were revealed in Makkah or Madinah. Click any surah to read its verses in Arabic with an English translation.

Who are the Prophets covered on this site?

From Adam and Nuh to Ibrahim, Musa, Isa, and Muhammad ﷺ — each profile includes a short biography and direct references to where their stories appear in the Quran.

How does the search feature work?

Simply type a name — such as "Khalid" or "Maryam" — on the Search page, and the site will return matching Prophets or Companions with summaries and references.

Is the content free to access?

Yes. All content on Islam Is The Way is free, intended for learning, reflection, and sharing knowledge of Islam with others.
